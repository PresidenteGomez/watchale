Watchale

One of the biggest problems in Minneapolis and around the Country is the lack of a centralized system to protect our immigrant communities from being separated and deported from their families. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agents lurk in our local communities and dismantle families from each other. These “ICE raids” are incredibly disruptive to communities. The application’s goal is to encourage neighbors to report ICE sightings, so families have the opportunity to stay together. Keeping our communities safe is the most important goal of this application.
